Our Vision

We aim to provide designers and clients with the freedom to create one-of-a-kind carpets based on one of our designs or their ideas. We think that by varying colour, weave, material, size, and pattern, we can create a highly individualized carpet that is as unique as the person who owns it. We want to work with several well-known wholesalers, merchants, and interior designers throughout the world.
